We save lives by empowering first responders to keep people breathing.

Our flagship product, the McKay Airway, is a proprietary medical device that simplifies airway management. It reduces the technical skills and training required to keep a patient's airway open. The device is effective with both unconcious and awake patients.

Our device falls under Class I Health Canada classification, and has successfully been used in trial procedures. We're open to inquries about the device related to potential use cases, clinical efficacy, or potential business discussions.
