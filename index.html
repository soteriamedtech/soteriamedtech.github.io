We save lives by empowering first responders to keep people breathing.

Our flagship product, the McKay Airway, is a proprietary medical device that simplifies airway management. It reduces the technical skills and training required to keep a patient's airway open. The device is effective with both unconcious and awake patients. Using the device results in higher success rates than the currently used methods.
